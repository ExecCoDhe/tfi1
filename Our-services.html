Specialist Service Provider for Teacher Recruitment Agencies

To ensure that only the most suitable teachers are put forward, we follow a meticulous and structured process:

  • ✅ Preliminary Interviews – Initial screening to assess qualifications, experience, and motivation.
  • ✅ Background Verification – Identity, address, academic qualifications, overseas police records, and employment & education history are meticulously checked.
  • ✅ Technical & Soft Skills Evaluation – Ensuring strong subject knowledge plus communication & adaptability skills.
  • ✅ Cultural & Professional Integration Assessment – Evaluating each candidate’s readiness for international education systems.
  • ✅ Application & Documentation Processing – Handling paperwork, forwarding application materials, certified documents, and evaluation remarks.
  • ✅ Reference Checks – Arranging and authenticating references to validate credibility.
  • ✅ Interview Coordination – Managing logistics for all necessary interviews between candidates and agencies.
  • ✅ Visa & Relocation Assistance – Providing guidance for a smooth visa process and relocation.

Flexible & Adaptable Approach

While we have a proven process, we understand each agency has unique needs. We are open to tailoring our approach to accommodate additional procedures that would benefit your hiring process.

Our Candidate Network

Candidates are our most valuable asset, bringing the expertise, skills, and potential needed to meet recruitment demands. We are fortunate to have a database of experienced and qualified teachers, made possible by our network of educational professionals.
